About Us

Welcome to Namma Kadai – your favorite neighborhood destination for delicious food and warm hospitality. Established in 2013, we have been proudly serving our customers for over 10 years with a commitment to quality, taste, and tradition.

What started as a humble eatery in the heart of Chennai has now grown into a thriving chain with 10+ franchises across major districts of Tamil Nadu including Coimbatore, Madurai, Trichy, and more.

At Namma Kadai, we specialize in both traditional and modern non-veg delicacies that reflect the rich culinary heritage of Tamil Nadu. Each dish is crafted with fresh ingredients, authentic spices, and lots of love.

Our mission is simple – to serve happiness on a plate. Whether you’re dining in, ordering out, or visiting any of our franchises, we ensure a consistent and satisfying food experience every time.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. We look forward to continuing to serve you the best – today, tomorrow, and always!
